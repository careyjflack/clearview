M.J. Blanks Dry Goods & Notions

The Blanks family ran a dry goods store that sold a little of everything — a quiet, essential presence in Clearview's commercial life through the 1910s.

History

In the 1910s N. and M. J. Blanks operated a groceries and dry goods store in Clearview.

The business did a little of everything, buying and selling produce and livestock, as well as operating a millinery. 1914 was the last mention of the store in the Clearview Patriarch.

May have relocated to Boley by the 1920s, or the siblings may have split up the business.
