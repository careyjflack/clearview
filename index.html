clearview historical mapping project
Est. 1903 · Okfuskee County, OK
Introduction

A record of the businesses and storefronts of one of Oklahoma's All-Black towns.

Founded in 1903, Clearview was among more than fifty All-Black towns established in Indian and Oklahoma Territory. This project catalogs its main street establishments — the merchants, churches, schools, and halls that made the town.
